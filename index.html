Welcome to Pedal’s documentation!

Pedal is a framework to analyze student’s code and provide feedback. Pedal is not only a collection of powerful program analysis tools, but is also built to give a model for what Feedback should be. Our goal is to elevate Feedback with Software Engineering and Instructional Design practices, to make it a central part of your course’s development rather than an afterthought.
